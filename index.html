Howdy,

Lucky Horse Rescue is a 501(c)3 non-profit, based in McMinnville, Tennessee. We take in abandoned, owner-surrendered, and abused horses. We provide them with the love and care they need to find a new home.

Our motto, “Helping Horses Help People," is what we stand by. We offer equine therapy. It provides a safe place for healing. It helps individuals with mental health issues, grief, loss, and more. It teaches the community how to care for horses. The keys to achieving this are education, love, kindness, hard work, and acceptance.

We have rehabilitated every one of our horses used in our community programs. If they can do it, so can you!

Adoptions Avaliable

Socks

An elderly woman surrendered Socks to our rescue because she could no longer care for him. Socks can be pushy on the ground, but has a very smooth ride. He rides English or Western, good for the farrier, loads and ties good. Barefoot and does not need shoes. Socks is rated for an intermediate rider.

Phoenix

Phoenix's owners surrendered him due to intermittent issues with bucking. He has undergone professional training to remedy the problem. He is easy to handle and rides well, making a great trail horse. Phoenix has a rating for an intermediate, experienced rider.

Casey

Casey’s owners became unable to keep up with her care and physical demands. She is sweet but reactive, buddy sour, cribs and can be hard to load. She is cold-backed and needs to be lunged before riding. She has received training in Western, English, and bareback riding. Casey is rated for an experienced rider.
